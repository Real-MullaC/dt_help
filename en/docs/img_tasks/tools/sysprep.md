# SysPrep Preparator

<p align="center">
	<img src="../../../logo.png" />
</p>

Sysprep Preparator is a Windows tool that helps allow you to prepare your system to allow you to capture an image for you to put into your ISO.

## Usage

1. Download SysPrep Preparator from the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/CodingWonders/SysprepPreparator)
2. Launch the exe in your image you are wanting to capture.
3. Go through the wizard and follow each step manually one by one.
